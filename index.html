PakStream – Stay Connected to Pakistan, Wherever You Are

Missing home? Whether you're sipping chai in Sweden, commuting in London, or relaxing in Toronto, PakStream is here to bring Pakistan closer to you.

Our platform makes it simple to watch Pakistani news online, listen to Urdu radio stations, and stay up-to-date with political talk shows and entertainment, all from one place. No VPNs. No hassle.
