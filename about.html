About PakStream

Hi, I'm Atif, a Pakistani living in Sweden and a lifelong software nerd. PakStream started as a small side project built late at night, during what I like to call "vibe coding" sessions, the kind where you're not chasing deadlines, just chasing the feeling of home.

Like many Pakistanis abroad, I missed the sound of FM radio, the drama of live TV news, and the honest voices of independent YouTubers. So I built a platform to bring all that together in one clean, ad-free space, just for people like me (and maybe you too).

PakStream is not backed by any company or commercial agenda. It's personal. A way for us to stay connected to Pakistan, no matter where we are in the world.

This blog and platform are my attempt to share my journey, connect with like-minded Pakistanis, and improve PakStream based on your feedback. If you have a suggestion, idea, or even just want to say salam, I'm all ears.
